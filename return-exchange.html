Return & Exchange Policy

At Munchies, customer satisfaction is our top priority. While we take great care in preparing and delivering your food, we understand that issues can occur. Below is our return and exchange policy:

Returns

Due to the perishable nature of food, we do not accept returns once an order has been delivered. However, if you receive an incorrect or spoiled item, please contact us within 1 hour of delivery for assistance.

Exchanges

If you receive a wrong item or an item that does not meet our quality standards, we will offer an exchange or store credit. The issue must be reported immediately with a photo of the item.

Eligibility for Exchange

  • Report must be made within 1 hour of delivery.
  • Photo evidence of the issue must be provided.
  • The item must be mostly untouched and not consumed.

Non-Returnable Items

We do not accept exchanges or returns for food that was delivered accurately and met our quality standards.

Contact Us

If you face any issues with your order, please contact our support team at: support@munchies.com or call +123 456 7890.
